In the Post-Digital era, the borders between production and consumption are more than ever blended: the aura of content seems to have vanished. However, these projects reflect ways of reintroducing the notion of authenticity in the reapropriation in cultural production. Here you can find the full book, which covers topics such as What is Authenticity?; Post-Digital = Loss of Authenticity? and The Ressurgence of Aura.
